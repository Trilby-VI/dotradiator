3 Tips to Maximize Your Car's A/C & Radiator

  1. Set temperature all the way cold or hot. Mixing wastes energy.
  2. Vent hot air first. Let the cabin heat escape before turning on A/C.
  3. Keep moving. Even 5–15 mph airflow keeps your A/C efficient and cools faster.
