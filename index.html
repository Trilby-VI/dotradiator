About Us

DOT Radiator is your trusted destination for classic radiator restoration and full-service cooling system repair. With decades of hands-on experience, we specialize in everything under the hood that keeps your engine cool—including water pumps, thermostats, fan belts, hoses, heater cores, and even old gas tanks with leaks and rust. Whether you’re restoring a classic car or maintaining a daily driver, we deliver the kind of care that only decades of experience can offer.
