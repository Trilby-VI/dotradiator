3 Tips to Maximize Your Car's A/C Quality & Radiator

  1. Pinning Dial Cold or Hot is Efficient
    On most cars, set the dial all blue for max cooling or all red for max heat. Mixing the dial wastes energy and doesn’t make the system more efficient.
  2. Let Hot Air Escape Before Asking the System to Cool the Cabin
    Let heat escape and set the fan to HIGH. The system is built to handle it - blasting hot air out first makes the AC work better.
  3. Move, Don’t Idle For Cold A/C
    Light driving, even 5–15+ mph—pushes fresh air across the radiator and condenser. Airflow makes AC chill faster and keeps engine temps steady.
